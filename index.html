About the Project

IndoorNav AI helps blind and low-vision individuals navigate complex buildings using AR glasses, audio feedback, and wearable technologies. From hospitals and airports to malls and offices, our solution ensures safe navigation, even during emergencies.

Key Stakeholders

  • Hospitals & Medical Centers
  • Airports & Train Stations
  • Municipal Buildings & Courthouses
  • Corporate Campuses
  • Shopping Malls
